MabuKing's

Calling

* * *

Indulging in alcohol and food are often seen in a negative light. We were staring at a feature wall in our kitchen (a “graveyard” of some alcohol bottles we had conquered in the past) when we were hit by an epiphany…

Why spend the mythical (& insufferable) 21 days to break our bad habits, when we can make the world a more vibrant place, and make everyone happy by introducing new flavour twists with alcohol and food.

This is MabuK.ing’s calling…
